Refund Policy

Effective date: 03/03/2026

Thank you for purchasing Vigor Max. We want you to feel confident trying our digital program. This page explains how refunds work.

Summary

  • 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee: You may request a full refund within 7 calendar days from your purchase date.
  • Refunds: Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
  • Digital Access: Upon refund approval, access to the product and related files may be revoked.

How to Request a Refund

Please contact our support team:

  1. Via Our Support: Email us at contatoleo@ngvdigital.site with your full name, order number, and purchase email. We will process your refund.

Eligibility Window

  • Refunds are available within 7 calendar days from the purchase date.
  • Requests made after 7 days are generally not eligible under this policy.
  • If applicable, statutory consumer withdrawal rights (e.g., EU/UK) remain unaffected; our 7-day window is a voluntary extension for your convenience.

What Is Covered

  • Vigor Max (digital guide and included files).
  • Duplicate purchases of the same product made in error.

What Is Not Covered

  • Purchases made via unauthorized resellers.
  • Orders where fraudulent or abusive activity is suspected (including excessive refund patterns or reselling of access files).
  • Any third-party tools or products not included in your order.

Digital Access on Refund

Our product is delivered digitally. When a refund is approved, your access may be disabled and any license to use the files is revoked. Please delete local copies after a refund is issued.

Processing Time & Method

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
  • Once approved, please allow 3–10 business days for your bank or card issuer to post the credit (timing may vary by country and provider).
  • Currency conversions and bank fees are controlled by your provider and are outside our control.

Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback, the order may be locked during the investigation. We recommend emailing us first at contatoleo@ngvdigital.site so we can help resolve your request faster under this Refund Policy.

EU/UK Right of Withdrawal

If you reside in the EU/UK, you may have a statutory right to withdraw from a digital content contract within 14 days unless you expressly consent to immediate delivery and acknowledge the loss of withdrawal rights. Our 7-day money-back guarantee is a voluntary benefit in addition to any mandatory rights you may have. Please contact us for specific instructions.
